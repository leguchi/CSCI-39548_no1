I say nothing is impossible. When people say "It's impossible to fly like a bird," I tell them "Nothing is impossible; It just hasn't happened yet." If they tell me how I am saying how "nothing" is the thing that is not possible, I don't disagree, but my belief is how anything can happen.

Character Meaning Example
^ Matching the starting input console.log("Alabama".match(/^A/)); logs "A" of "Alabama"
$ Matching the final input console.log("Bob".match(/b$/)); logs "b" of "Bob"
\b Matching a word boundary console.log("Crab".match(/rab\b/)); logs "rab" of "Crab"
\B Matching a non-word boundary console.log("Crab".match(/Cr\B/)); logs "Cr" of "Crab"1
